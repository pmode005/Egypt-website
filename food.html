Koshary

Koshary is Egypt’s quintessential comfort food and a legendary street-food staple that is as unique as it is satisfying. It is a hearty, vegan-friendly carb-fest that layers lentils, rice, and macaroni, which are then topped with a spiced tomato sauce and a signature garnish of crispy fried onions.

Ful

Ful medames, or simply ful, is often considered the true national dish of Egypt, serving as a protein-packed staple that fuels millions of people every day. It consists of fava beans that are slow-cooked in a large, pot-bellied metal jar called a qidra until they reach a creamy, tender consistency.
